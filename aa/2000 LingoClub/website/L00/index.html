Back Story.

This site is an on going sketch of my class in Game Design and Programming for teens at the Eastmont Computing Center.

The goal is to enable students to take success in game play and extend that to excellence in school, work, and life.

In the class we'll use the Macromedia Director software and the Lingo computer programming language to build animation, interactive art, and games. I am the inventor of the Lingo programming language and I've spent over a decade developing it for Macromedia.
